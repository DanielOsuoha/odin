Cheese and Eggs

Description

Cheese and eggs is a simple yet delicious dish that combines the creamy richness of cheese with the versatile and nutritious qualities of eggs. Typically prepared by cooking eggs in various styles such as scrambled, fried, or poached, and then adding grated or melted cheese for added flavor and texture. This dish is enjoyed for its comforting taste and can be served for breakfast, brunch, or any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Cook the beaten eggs in a skillet until almost set.
  3. Sprinkle grated cheese over the eggs.
  4. Cook until the cheese is melted and the eggs are fully cooked.
