Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Brown the ground beef or Italian sausage in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain excess fat.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the cooked ground beef or sausage with marinara sauce until well combined.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta cheese, repeating until all ingredients are used up. End with a layer of meat sauce on top.
